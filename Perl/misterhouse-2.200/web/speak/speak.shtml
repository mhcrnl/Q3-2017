MisterHouse the DJ

If you have a shoutcast compatable mp3 player (e.g. Winamp, Xmms, or Windows Media Player), point it to http://music.misterhouse.net to listen to what our house is saying, with about a 10 second lag. In addition to echoing what is being said in our house, the 'House DJ' will make random comments twice a minute. Background music is usually home grown Bruce Songs.

The various voices are generated from either the AT&T Natural Voices or Cepstral TTS speech engines, available on both Linux and Windows.

Feel free to read a deep thought, or a tagline, review what was recently spoken, or type something in below.
