Articles and websites about MisterHouse

  • We have a wiki at http://misterhouse.wikispaces.com for user-supplied documentation. You can use rss or atom feeds to track changes to the wiki.
  • Tyrel McMahan posted a podcast interview with Bruce about Misterhouse on his radio show.
  • David Lynch wrote an linuxjournel article on how to automate zoned radiant heat system
  • The CaFe Perl Users Group has an online Interview with Bruce Winter, one of the MisterHouse authors. Another interview can be found on cocoontech.com which is summarized over on itbusinessedge.com
  • Wally Kissel has a presentation on MisterLab which shows how he uses MisterHouse at Fermi Labs.
  • Neil Cherry wrote Linux Smart Homes For Dummies which has lots of good Home Automation projects for Linux, including some chapters on MisterHouse.
  • Thomas Vogl has a 'Misterhouse Journal' where he cronicles his experiences of setting up MisterHouse on Linux Redhat.
  • Pete Flaherty has a MrAudrey website with a mh compatable Audrey image, along with useful Audrey related notes.
  • Circuit Cellar Ink article from October 99.
  • Brian's page has a nice article on "The MisterHouse APRS Tracking System (MHATS)", using MisterHouse and Ham radios to track vehicles.
  • The 10/00 Wired Magazine has an article about the Perl Whirl, a geekcruise.com trip up to Alaska. It doesn't have that much to do with MisterHouse, but it was a fun trip and it is an interesting article about Perl authors and users.
  • NewsForge mentioned MisterHouse in an article debating open source programing on Windows.
  • Slashdot had a post on MisterHouse on May 2003.
  • Gordon Meyer has a nice intro article at OReilly ONLamp, posted November 2004.
